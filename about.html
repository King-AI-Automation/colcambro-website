About

Colin Campbell

Colin Campbell helps business owners generate more leads, convert more opportunities, and build predictable revenue systems through practical sales coaching, consulting, and training.

Before launching his own business, Colin spent years leading sales teams as a Sales Director, building the processes, habits, and frameworks that drive consistent revenue growth. In his last corporate role, he personally delivered over £2.9M in revenue at a 49% profit margin from new business to the firm.

Today, he works with business owners across a range of sectors including property, professional services, fitness, financial services and other service-based industries, helping them move from inconsistent pipelines and referral dependence to structured, repeatable growth.

In 2020, Colin launched CamBro Conversations, a podcast that has grown into a top 1% globally ranked show with more than 345 episodes and listeners in 121 countries. Through conversations with entrepreneurs, investors, athletes, psychologists, and industry leaders, he has explored the principles behind high performance in business and life.

At the centre of Colin's work is a simple philosophy: Brain, Body & Business. The belief that business performance is rarely just about strategy. How you think, how you manage your energy, and how you execute consistently all influence the results you achieve.

Whether coaching a founder, delivering a workshop, or hosting a podcast conversation, the goal remains the same: helping people perform at a higher level and create sustainable success.
