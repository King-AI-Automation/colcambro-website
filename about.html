About

Colin Campbell

Colin spent years as a Sales Director, leading teams and building the systems that generate consistent revenue. When he went all-in on coaching and consulting, he brought everything he'd learned with him.

His coaching and training work spans property, professional services, fitness, and financial services: sectors that look different on the surface but share the same sales fundamentals. The businesses that grow are the ones that take those fundamentals seriously.

In 2020 he launched CamBro Conversations, a podcast that quickly reached the top 1% globally and now has 345+ episodes, listened to in 121 countries. The show covers business, mindset, performance, and everything in between. Guests have included Chris Williamson of Modern Wisdom.

Outside of work, Colin's focus is Brain, Body & Business: the belief that how you think, how you look after yourself physically, and how you build your business are all the same conversation.
