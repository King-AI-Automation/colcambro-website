Speaking topics

All sessions are tailored to the audience and format. Topics can be combined or run as standalone keynotes, workshops, or panel contributions.

01

The Art of Consultative Selling

How asking the right questions, and genuinely listening, leads to more sales, faster. The counterintuitive approach to closing that actually builds trust.

02

Building a Personal Brand That Attracts Leads

LinkedIn, podcasting, and content strategy for professionals who want inbound enquiries instead of cold outreach. What actually works in 2026.

03

Brain, Body & Business

The performance principles behind top-1% achievers. How physical, mental, and professional habits compound, and why most high-earners are leaving results on the table.

04

The Follow-Up Formula

The majority of deals are lost in the follow-up, not the pitch. A practical framework for staying front-of-mind without being annoying, and converting interest into action.

05

Designing a Predictable Sales System

How to move from revenue that depends on who's in the building to a process that works consistently. Frameworks any business owner can implement immediately.

06

The Sales Mindset

What separates top performers from average ones is rarely skills. It's how they think about rejection, effort, and identity. A talk that reframes selling for every professional who thinks they "hate sales."
